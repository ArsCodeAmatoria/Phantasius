# Corrected Mathematical Expressions

## Matrix and Vector Notation

A simple matrix:

$$\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}$$

The identity matrix:

$$I = \begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}$$

A vector in quantum mechanics:

$$|\psi\rangle = \alpha|0\rangle + \beta|1\rangle$$

Where $|\alpha|^2 + |\beta|^2 = 1$.
